Home Rooms

"At F5 Hotel, we believe that true luxury lies in the details and genuine hospitality."

Welcome to F5 Hotel, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Makati City. Located in the prestigious Ayala Center, our hotel offers a perfect blend of modern elegance and warm Filipino hospitality. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience, whether you're visiting for business or leisure.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our service. From our spacious, well-appointed rooms to our world-class amenities including a stunning pool, relaxing sauna, and fine dining restaurant, we ensure that every moment of your stay is nothing short of exceptional. At F5 Hotel, your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.
